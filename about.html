Our Story

We are a warm and inviting eatery located in the heart of the city, serving up a delicious range of freshly baked goods, hearty breakfast options, and handcrafted coffee drinks. Our menu is made from only the finest ingredients, and is constantly evolving to reflect the seasons and our customers' changing tastes.

Our Mission

We are committed to using only the finest ingredients in our baked goods and beverages, sourcing locally and seasonally whenever possible. Our team of skilled bakers and baristas are passionate about their craft, and take pride in creating products that not only taste amazing, but also look beautiful.

At the heart of our mission is a dedication to community. We believe that food has the power to bring people together, and we aim to be a gathering place for neighbors, friends, and families. Whether you're grabbing a quick coffee on the way to work or settling in for a leisurely brunch with loved ones, we hope that you'll find Cherry Lane Cafe & Bakery to be a place where you feel at home.

Thank you for choosing Cherry Lane Cafe & Bakery as your go-to spot for baked goods and coffee. We look forward to serving you and being a part of your daily routine!

Our Defining Qualities

  • High-quality ingredients sourced from local and sustainable suppliers
  • Passionate team dedicated to the art of coffee-making and culinary delights
  • Commitment to excellence in every aspect of the business
  • Active involvement in the local community
  • Warm and inviting atmosphere
  • Positive presence in the neighborhood
  • Attention to detail and customer experience.
